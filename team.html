Meet Our Team

Our goal is to find people who are talented at teaching, can motivate people to learn Language and are able to connect and empathise with people. Fluency in English, Spanish, French, Nepali and Dutch, a dynamic personality, professionalism, empathy and passion for the job are the defining characteristics of the teachers on our team.
